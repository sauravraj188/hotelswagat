Welcome to Hotel Swagat

About Hotel Swagat

Hotel Swagat, located in the historic town of Saharanpur, Uttar pradesh, is more than just a place to stay; it’s an experience that blends modern comfort with the rich heritage of the region. Our mission is to provide a welcoming and memorable stay for every guest, whether you’re here for business, leisure, or to explore the cultural treasures of Saharanpur.

With a commitment to impeccable service, comfortable accommodations, and a delightful dining experience, we aim to be your home away from home in this enchanting part of Uttar Pradesh.

We look forward to hosting you and creating lasting memories during your visit to Saharanpur.
